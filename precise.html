Personalized Medicine

Overview: A holistic approach that considers patient-specific factors for better care.

  • Patient demographics: Age, gender, ethnicity.
  • Disease characteristics: Symptoms, progression.
  • Individual history: Personal medical and lifestyle factors.
Precision Medicine

Focus: Leveraging genetic and molecular insights for targeted therapies.

  • Patient demographics: Age, gender, lifestyle.
  • Disease characteristics: Diagnosis, severity, comorbidities.
  • Biomarkers: Genetic and biochemical indicators.
  • Molecular information: DNA sequencing, cellular analysis.
Precision Care

Advanced Approach: Incorporating social, economic, and personal goals for optimized care.

  • Patient demographics: Age, gender, and family health history.
  • Disease characteristics: Unique responses to treatments.
  • Biomarkers & molecular data: High-tech insights for precision.
  • Preferences and goals: Patient values, desired outcomes.
  • Social determinants: Income, education, access to care.
